# INTUNE Lore, Storyline, Sprites, and Background Direction

## Storyline

Long ago, the sea was regulated by the **Harmonic Tides**, currents tuned by the Mermaid Queen through coral resonators. A deep-ocean entity called **The Detuner** shattered those resonators with dissonant waves. To stop a total collapse, the Queen sealed herself in a harmonic prism.

You are a surface-born diver known as a **Pitchfinder**, one of the few who can still hear true intervals underwater. Guided by surviving sea archivists, you descend through five regions to restore cadence anchors and free the Queen.

## Region Lore

1. **Reef District (Beginner)**
   - Former cultural center of reef musicians.
   - Bright, optimistic palette with damaged but repairable structures.
   - Teaches clean tension-to-resolution language (ii–V–I).

2. **Sunken Shipyard (Intermediate)**
   - Graveyard of transport vessels converted to jazz workshops.
   - Blues-influenced side quests and backdoor resolutions.
   - NPCs include octopus percussionist engineers.

3. **Kelp Cathedral (Intermediate+)**
   - Sacred resonance chambers grown from giant kelp pipes.
   - Modal harmony and suspended tension mechanics.
   - Choir-like whale spirits act as puzzle hints.

4. **Abyssal Trench (Advanced)**
   - Minimal light and heavy pressure distort rhythm windows.
   - Tritone substitution and chromatic voice-leading challenges.
   - Hostile detuned predators evolve from corrupted guardians.

5. **Queen's Vault (Finale)**
   - Prism chamber where all motifs return.
   - Multi-phase confrontation with The Detuner.
   - Final puzzle is a long-form progression and rhythm duel.

## Main Characters

- **Player (The Pitchfinder)**: agile diver wielding a resonance staff.
- **Mermaid Queen Neris**: imprisoned conductor of Harmonic Tides.
- **The Detuner**: abyssal sound-eater that thrives on unresolved dissonance.
- **Archivist Tull**: turtle historian who teaches harmonic mechanics.

## Sprite Direction

- **Player Sprite**
  - 24x24 or 32x32.
  - Animations: idle float, swim, cast, hit, purified-victory pose.
  - Accent color pulse tied to successful cadence timing.

- **Detuned Enemies**
  - Cracked glowing seams and jitter offset for unstable tuning.
  - State swaps: normal -> detuned glow -> stunned/purified (smooth textures).

- **Chord Gate / Resonance Totem**
  - Coral arch with orbiting note glyphs.
  - Color shifts by harmonic state:
    - Red = unresolved
    - Amber = partially correct
    - Cyan = correctly tuned

## Background/Environment Direction

Use layered parallax in each room:

1. Deep gradient backdrop.
2. Slow macro bubbles.
3. Mid coral silhouette layer.
4. Foreground kelp sway with sine animation.

Suggested room mood progression:
- Reef: turquoise + sunlight shafts.
- Shipyard: navy + rust tones + drifting debris.
- Cathedral: emerald + fog bloom.
- Abyss: near-black + bioluminescent accents.
- Vault: royal cyan + prism beams.

## Audio Direction

- Exploration: soft brushes, upright bass, delayed electric piano.
- Puzzle active: metronome + filtered comping.
- Correct cadence: bright major-6/9 stab.
- Wrong cadence: dissonant cluster with underwater low-pass tail.
